Our Story · Made in Delhi

Makhana, made better.

India's oldest superfood — handpicked from the lotus ponds of Bihar and slow-roasted in cold-pressed oil, never fried. This is The Makhana.

Every great snack begins with respect for its ingredient. Ours begins with a single lotus seed.

For generations, families in Bihar have waded into the lotus ponds at first light, gathering foxnuts by hand — a craft older than most of the food on our shelves.

We started The Makhana to carry that craft forward, honestly — to take a humble, quiet seed and give it the care it has always deserved.

So we keep it simple. Handpicked, slow-roasted in cold-pressed oil, sealed fresh. Never deep-fried. Never drowned in palm oil. Nothing to hide on the label.

Light enough for an everyday handful. Good enough for a festive gift. Honest enough that you never think twice.
