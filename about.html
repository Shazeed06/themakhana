01 / Who we are

A modern snacking brand, rooted in Madhubani heritage.

We are a small team obsessed with one humble lotus seed — and the centuries of craft behind it.

Long before makhana became a wellness headline, families across Madhubani, Bihar were wading into lotus ponds at dawn to gather foxnuts by hand. That tradition is the heart of everything we make. We work directly with these farming communities, paying fairly and sourcing seasonally, so the people who grow our makhana share in its success.

Every batch is cleaned, graded and roasted in cold-pressed oil — never deep-fried, never drowned in palm oil. What reaches your bowl is exactly what nature intended: a light, protein-rich, gluten-free, non-GMO snack that fits an unapologetically modern life.

We built The Makhana on three simple pillars: authentic Bihar roots, premium quality, and modern healthy snacking — and we refuse to compromise on any of them.

03 / Our heritage

The heritage of Madhubani.

Madhubani is more than a place on a map — it is the makhana capital of the world, where lotus ponds and folk artistry have shaped life for centuries. We are proud to build our brand on that legacy.

We work hand-in-hand with local farming communities, honouring centuries-old methods of harvesting and curing while investing in sustainable livelihoods that let these traditions thrive for generations to come.

07 / Good to know

Questions, answered.
