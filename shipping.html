Shipping Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

At The Makhana, we want your foxnuts to reach you fresh, sealed and on time. This Shipping Policy explains how and where we deliver, what it costs, and what to expect once you place an order. We are based in Delhi, India, and dispatch every order from there.

Shipping Coverage

We ship pan-India. Orders are delivered to all serviceable PIN codes across the country through our trusted courier partners. If a delivery address falls outside a courier's serviceable area, our team will contact you to arrange an alternative or process a refund.

At present we ship within India only and do not offer international delivery.

Processing Time

Orders are usually processed and dispatched within 1-2 business days of being placed. Business days are Monday to Saturday, excluding public holidays. Orders placed on a Sunday or a holiday are processed on the next working day.

Delivery Time

Once dispatched, orders typically arrive within 3-7 business days, depending on your location. Metro cities are usually quicker, while remote or non-metro PIN codes may take a little longer.

  • Metro and nearby regions: around 3-5 business days
  • Remote or non-metro locations: around 5-7 business days

These timelines are estimates and begin after your order has been dispatched, not from the moment it is placed.

Shipping Charges

We offer free shipping on all prepaid orders above Rs.599. For orders below this value, a flat shipping fee of Rs.49 applies. The applicable shipping charge, if any, is shown clearly at checkout before you confirm your order.

Order Tracking

As soon as your order is dispatched, we will email you a tracking link and the courier details so you can follow your parcel's journey. If you have not received tracking information within 3 business days of placing your order, please write to us at hello@themakhana.in and we will help.

Cash on Delivery

Cash on Delivery (COD) is available on most serviceable PIN codes across India. Availability is confirmed at checkout based on your delivery address. Where COD is offered, you can pay in cash, or by UPI or card if the delivery partner supports it, at the time of delivery. A small COD handling fee may apply and will be shown at checkout.

Delays

While we work hard to deliver on time, some delays are beyond our control. Deliveries may occasionally take longer due to courier disruptions, severe weather, regional restrictions, or high demand during festivals and sale periods. If your order is significantly delayed, please reach out and we will follow up with the courier on your behalf.

Incorrect Address

Please double-check your shipping address and contact number before confirming your order. We are not responsible for delays or non-delivery caused by an incomplete or incorrect address. If you spot a mistake soon after ordering, email us at hello@themakhana.in as quickly as possible and we will try to update the details before dispatch. Orders returned to us due to an incorrect address may attract a re-shipping charge.

Contact

Have a question about your order or our shipping? We are happy to help. Email us at hello@themakhana.in or reach out on Instagram @themakhanaofficial. We usually respond within 1-2 business days.
