Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

The Makhana ("we", "us", "our") is a direct-to-consumer brand based in Delhi, India, selling roasted and raw makhana (foxnuts) through our website at themakhana.in. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect when you visit our website or place an order, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website, you agree to the practices described here.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information we need to process your orders and run our business. This may include:

  • Your name and the name of anyone you are shipping to.
  • Contact details such as your email address and phone number.
  • Your shipping and billing address.
  • Order information, such as the products you buy and your order history.
  • Basic technical details such as your device type and pages visited, collected through cookies (see below).

We do not store your debit card, credit card or UPI details. All payments are handled directly by our payment partners on secure systems, and card details never reach our servers.

How We Use It

We use your information to:

  • Process, pack and deliver your orders.
  • Send order confirmations, shipping updates and delivery notifications.
  • Respond to your questions and provide customer support.
  • Send you offers and updates, but only if you have chosen to receive them.
  • Improve our website, products and overall service.
  • Meet our legal and accounting obligations.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to remember your cart, keep you signed in, and understand how visitors use the site. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings. Please note that some parts of the website may not work as expected if cookies are turned off.

Sharing With Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted partners who help us run our business, and only as far as needed to do so. These include:

  • Courier and logistics partners, to deliver your order to your address.
  • Payment partners, to securely process your payment.
  • Service providers who support our website, email and analytics.

We may also disclose information if required to do so by law or by a valid request from a public authority.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information using appropriate technical and organisational measures. Payments are processed over secure, encrypted connections. While no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, we work to keep your data safe and limit access to it.

Your Rights

You have the right to access the personal information we hold about you, to ask us to correct it if it is inaccurate, and to request that we delete it where there is no ongoing need to keep it. To make any such request, please email us at themakhana.official@gmail.com and we will respond within a reasonable time.

Children's Privacy

Our website and products are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with their details, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your information, please email us at themakhana.official@gmail.com. The Makhana is based in Delhi, India.
