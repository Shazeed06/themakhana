The Origin Story

From Bihar's ponds to your desk.

Before sunrise in Madhubani, Bihar, farming families wade into lotus ponds and gather foxnuts by hand — the same way their grandparents did. It's a craft older than almost anything on a supermarket shelf, and it deserves better than a deep fryer. That's where we come in.

We bring those handpicked seeds to Delhi, slow-roast them in cold-pressed oil, and seal every batch fresh. No palm oil. No maida. No shortcuts. Just an honest, high-protein crunch that moves easily from your work desk to the festive gift box — and never apologises for either.
