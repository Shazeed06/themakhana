A chef co-founder's note — why a roasted (never fried) gift box stays crunchy & satvik

Our chef co-founder is firm on one thing: a gift box is only worth giving if it is still crisp when it is opened. Is makhana a good Rakhi gift?

Is a makhana gift box diabetic-safe to give an elder?

Roasted, unsweetened makhana is a low-glycaemic, high-fibre snack, so a plain or lightly spiced makhana gift box is generally a far more diabetic-friendly rakhi gift than a sweets box that is mostly sugar and syrup. Choose raw or lightly seasoned flavours rather than any sugar-coated variety, and keep portions to a small handful. This is general nutrition guidance, not medical advice, so an elder managing diabetes should always follow their doctor's plan.

What is the shelf life of makhana for shipping a Rakhi gift?

Well-roasted, properly sealed makhana typically stays crunchy for several months, which makes it ideal for shipping a Raksha Bandhan gift ahead of time. Our packs are sealed to hold freshness in transit, so a box ordered a week or two before Rakhi arrives crisp. Once opened, keep it in an airtight jar and it stays fresh for weeks, far longer than a fresh-sweets box that must be eaten within days.
