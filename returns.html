Return & Refunds Policy

Last updated: 22 June 2026

The Makhana is a food brand based in Delhi, India. As we sell sealed, ready-to-eat snacks, our return policy is shaped by food safety and hygiene rules. This page explains when a return or refund applies, how to raise a request, and how long it takes.

Our Promise

We want every pouch to reach you fresh and intact. If your order arrives damaged, incorrect, or spoiled, we will make it right with a replacement or a refund. Please read the eligibility details below so we can resolve your request quickly.

Eligibility

Because our products are food items, returns are accepted only in specific cases. You may raise a request if:

  • The product arrived damaged or with leaking, torn or broken packaging.
  • You received the wrong item or an incorrect quantity compared to your order.
  • The product is spoiled, expired, or quality-defective on arrival.

All such issues must be reported within 7 days of delivery, along with clear photographs of the product, the packaging and the batch or expiry details.

For hygiene and safety reasons, sealed food items that are correct and undamaged are generally non-returnable. We also cannot accept returns for items that have been opened, partially consumed, or stored incorrectly after delivery, unless the product was defective on arrival.

How to Raise a Request

To start a return or refund request, email us at themakhana.official@gmail.com within 7 days of delivery and include:

  • Your Order ID.
  • A short description of the issue.
  • Clear photos of the product, the outer and inner packaging, and the batch or expiry label.

Our team will review the details and respond, usually within 2 business days, with the next steps.

Refund Method & Timeline

Once your request is approved, you may choose either a replacement of the affected item or a refund.

Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5 to 7 business days. Depending on your bank or payment provider, it may take a little longer for the amount to reflect in your account. Shipping charges, where applicable, are refunded only when the return is due to our error.

Cancellations

You can cancel an order before it has been dispatched. To cancel, email themakhana.official@gmail.com with your Order ID as soon as possible. Once an order has been dispatched, it can no longer be cancelled, but it may still qualify under the eligibility conditions above if it arrives damaged, wrong or spoiled.

Exchanges

We offer exchanges only for items that arrive damaged, incorrect or defective. In these cases we will replace the affected product with the same item, subject to availability. We do not offer exchanges based on flavour or taste preference once a sealed pack has been opened.

Contact

For any question about returns, refunds or cancellations, write to us at themakhana.official@gmail.com or message us on Instagram at @themakhanaofficial. We are based in Delhi, India, and are happy to help.
